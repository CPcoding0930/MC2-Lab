Welcome to the Center for Metaverse and Computational Creativity (MC2) Lab led by Professor Pan Hui, a pioneering research hub dedicated to driving innovation in the frontiers of metaverse technology and ubiquitous computing.

At MC2, our mission is to push the boundaries of what's possible in the convergence of virtual reality (VR), augmented reality (AR), extended reality (XR), and mixed reality (MR) , as well as other transformative technologies shaping the metaverse. Through rigorous research, cross-disciplinary collaboration, and a commitment to pioneering discoveries, we are paving the way for the next generation of immersive experiences. Through rigorous interdisciplinary research, we are unlocking new possibilities in areas such as immersive vision, blockchain-based digital identities/humans, AI education (Metaverse Classroom and digital teachers), and the seamless integration of the physical and virtual worlds. Our work aims to redefine the boundaries of human-computer interaction and create captivating experiences that transcend the confines of traditional computing.
